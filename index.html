Represent, Educate, Campaign, Socialise, Liberate

Kingston University LGBT+ exists to create a non-discriminatory safe space for and bring together Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, any and all individuals who self-define into any marginalised sexual/romantic orientation or gender identity.

We aim to represent, and provide a louder voice to the LGBT+ community both within the University & Student Union, educate, provide awareness on LGBT+ issues, and running student-led events & campaigns to liberate and improve LGBT+ experiece here at Kingston
